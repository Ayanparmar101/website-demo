About Us

Welcome to our school's student admission process. We are dedicated to providing a quality education and nurturing environment for all our students.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Proof of age (birth certificate or passport)
  • Previous academic records
  • Recommendation letters

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form
  2. Submit the required documents
  3. Wait for the admission committee's review
  4. Receive the admission decision
  5. Complete the enrollment process
